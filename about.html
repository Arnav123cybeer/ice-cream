New adventure in ice cream

Ice cream is a beloved frozen dessert made primarily from cream, milk, sugar, and various flavors, evolving from its ancient origins in China and the Middle East to become a global staple. There are different types, such as regular ice cream, gelato, sorbet, and frozen yogurt, each with unique characteristics. With a rich history and fun facts like the ice cream cone's rise in popularity in 1904 and vanilla being the top flavor in the U.S., ice cream remains a symbol of celebration and joy in many cultures.
