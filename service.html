New adventure in ice cream

Scoopiee is a charming ice cream store that offers a delightful range of artisanal flavors made from locally sourced ingredients, including seasonal specials and options for dietary restrictions like dairy-free and vegan. Customers can enjoy a personalized experience by customizing their treats with various toppings and mix-ins, or indulging in sundaes and shakes. The welcoming atmosphere, friendly staff, and community engagement through local partnerships and charity events make Scoopiee a favorite spot for families and friends, while convenient takeout and delivery options ensure that everyone can enjoy their delicious offerings anywhere.
